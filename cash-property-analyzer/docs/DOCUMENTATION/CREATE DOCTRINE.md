# INVESTMENT DOCTRINE

## CORE MANDATE

This app evaluates **100% cash-only** residential multi‑let property deals in South Africa and ranks them by **risk‑adjusted owner earnings quality**.

## NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES (ENCODED IN CODE)

1. **No leverage**: No debt, no refinancing assumptions
2. **No IRR**: No internal rate of return calculations
3. **No overrides**: Auto-reject gates cannot be bypassed
4. **Cash-only analysis only**: No financing scenarios

## ABSOLUTE AUTO-REJECT GATES

A deal is rejected immediately if **any** apply:

- OEY < 12%
- Payback > 12 years
- Negative cash flow under combined stress
- Yield premium vs stocks < +3%

## SCORING FORMULA (LOCKED)

| Component                 | Weight | Purpose                |
| ------------------------- | ------ | ---------------------- |
| Owner Earnings Yield      | 30%    | Primary return metric  |
| Payback Period            | 20%    | Capital recovery speed |
| Stress-Test Survival      | 25%    | Worst-case resilience  |
| Expense Quality & Realism | 10%    | Operating efficiency   |
| Deal Simplicity & Control | 10%    | Execution risk         |
| Stock Yield Premium       | 5%     | Opportunity cost       |

## STRESS TESTS (MANDATORY)

Each deal must survive:

1. **Vacancy shock**: +20% vacancy rate
2. **Municipal increase**: +15% rates & utilities
3. **Maintenance surge**: 2× maintenance reserve

**Worst-case result** determines stress score.

## CAPITAL ALLOCATION PHILOSOPHY

> "Capital is allocated only to assets that produce observable owner earnings today, recover capital quickly under conservative assumptions, and outperform passive alternatives by a wide margin. Survival precedes growth. Compounding is allowed only after safety is assured."
